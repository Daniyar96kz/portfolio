Hej! I'm landscape photographer

My name is Daniyar Kaliyev and I am a landscape and nature photographer based out of Kazakhstan. I first fell in love with photography over half a lifetime ago as a young teenager after bringing my father's old FED-5V 2,8/55mm camera on a family trip to Katon-Karagai National Nature Park. After passing middle school I reignited my passion for photography while at the same time falling in love with the landscape here. In particular, the image of beautiful old pine trees clinging to rocky mountaintops stoked my imagination and has become a bit of an obsession for me.

I would describe my photographic style as light and moody. In general, my images are bright, often employing color as an important element. Often being more associated with darker imagery, mood is something I am constantly seeking out in the landscape: fog filled valleys, twisting pine groves and isolated dead trees are subjects often found at the other end of my lens. Compositionally, I mostly explore the fine line between simple and boring. A final note on image processing: clearly, my images are not straight out the camera jpg images. Mostly, I employ contrast and color edits through luminosity masks in an attempt to accentuate mood and evoke feelings and memories. That being said, I try to avoid processing for the sole sake of visual stimulation.
